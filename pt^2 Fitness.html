Welcome to pt² fitness

pt² fitness project aims to bring young people from refugee backgrounds to a place where they can learn how to box. This project is not only designed to enhance their access to sport but also to give them a sense of confidence and to unleash their potential.

pt² fitness was founded by Percy Tussamba, a competitive amateur boxer for almost seven years, and has recently been working with Fight For Peace as a coach, where he started as a youngster. Alongside his studies in Biomedical Engineering, he is now focused on shaping his vision and introducing the remarkable pt² fitness to the Newham Youth Partnership.

Percy wishes to raise awareness about the challenges faced by young refugees and support those who are struggling with their immigration process. Additionally, he aims to secure resources and build partnerships that will help sustain and expand pt² fitness.
